Privacy policy

Welcome to Shiny Star Beauty Parlour. Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our beauty services.

---

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information: Personal Details: Name, phone number, email address, and appointment details when you book or contact us. Service Information: Details about the services you book or inquire about. Website Usage Data: Information such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited (collected automatically to improve our site). ---

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: Schedule and manage appointments. Provide beauty and salon services. Respond to your inquiries and requests. Send promotional offers or updates (only if you have agreed). Improve our website and customer experience. ---

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Your information is only accessed by authorized staff for service-related purposes.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share your information only: With trusted service providers who help us operate our business (like booking or payment systems). When required by law or government authorities. ---

5. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer. ---

6. Your Rights

You have the right to: Access, update, or correct your personal data. Request deletion of your data (subject to legal or business requirements). Withdraw consent for marketing messages at any time. ---
